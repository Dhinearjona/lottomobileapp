The Philippines first Lotto Mobile Application. As per the survey, 10 out of 10 people would love to play Lotto using their mobile phones for one obvious reason - convenience. This app will allow them to play at the convenience of their homes, with higher hopes and chances of winning. The App will also generate additional revenue for the PCSO giving the Filipino people more programs and benefits for health, medical assistance, and charities.

How to use PCSO Lotto Mobile Application

Using the PCSO Lotto Mobile App is easy. Just visit the Google Play Store or Appstore and download the PCSO Lotto Mobile App now! Players must be at least 18yrs old. Just fill out the registration, top up your wallet, click the choice of your game and you're ready to play!
